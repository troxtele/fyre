MISSION AND VISION

Frederik Giguere is the founder and CEO of Fyre Logistics, a leading logistics and transportation company. Frederik's vision for his business is to create innovative solutions that streamline businesses’ supply chains for maximum efficiency and cost savings, while maintaining superior customer service standards at all times. To achieve this goal, he invests heavily in advanced technologies such as customer relationship management and tracking technologies, which are used both internally by his staff as well as externally by customers who rely on their services. Our company provides customers with express delivery, tracking options, online ordering systems, warehousing services and secure payment processing. Our team consists of experienced professionals who are dedicated to meeting customer demands while ensuring quality service at all times. Our Ceo is committed to finding new ways to improve operations and reduce costs through technology advances and process optimization. He works hard every day to ensure that his clients get the best possible service from his organization each time they avail its services.

With head offices in Québec, Canada we employ a far-reaching global network that stretches across five continents.

Our service is always fast and reliable, whether you need to ship your cargo across towns, provinces, or nations.
